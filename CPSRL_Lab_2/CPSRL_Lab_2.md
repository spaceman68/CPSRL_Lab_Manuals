## Introduction:


The purpose of this lab is to calibrate and level the granite table for experiments in the lab. This is necessary to ensure that air-bearing vehicles can operate on the granite surface without a gravity component causing the vehicle to have a bias in a given direction. (see figure X)

This experiment outlines a method to determine the slope and level of the table, as well as corrective action necessary to remedy the slope of the table.
